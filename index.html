Internet and the World Wide Web

What is the Internet

A common misconception is that the internet and the world wide web are the same thing. Nope. They are not. They are two completely different things that work together to provide the various tools and technology we enjoy.

First of, the internet existed before the world wide web. The internet is simply a network that links different devices together. Your computer, smart phone, tablet or any other internet capable device (your refrigerator, doorbell) to connects to the internet with a unique identifier. This makes the device able to send and receive email, text messages and other communication through the internet. In the early days of the internet, before smart phones and advanced devices, all this was done through simple computer terminals. Simple terminals served as clients that would communicate through the internet to more powerful servers. Until the next digital revolution arrived to take advantage of this infrastructure
