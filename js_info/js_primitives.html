JavaScript Primitives

What are Javascript primitves

Primitives are the smallest, individual unit of data. It could be someone's first name, age or if it's raining outside (true or false).

Types of primitives

Strings

For non programmers, strings can be for guitars, kites and quantum theory. I've had a student mention string cheese. In javascript and most programming languages (check to be sure), any characters or text contained in single or double quotes will be considered a string. Called string because it is a sequence of letters orignially referred to as a "string of characters"j.

Numbers

The amount or how much of something (quantity/price) is usually expressed in numerical fashion. Numbers are primitives we could used math on. Using math on strings is silly.
