Paragraphs and line breaks

Probably the most basic and most used tag is the p tag. Thats what we have for the majority of HTML text content.

HTML even has markup identifying where the browser should have a line break, this is the br tag. Worth noting that the br tag similar to the img tag is closed by a slash right before the greater than sign of the br tag.
See sample below

<br />

Such was the concern for the sanctity and inviolate nature of some text, a markup to ensure that the space between two elements/content be always side by side. A good example of this is the name "de la Paz". By having the appropriate markup between de and la and between la and Paz, it will treat that last name as one unit instead of three separate potential lines. See code sample below.

de&nbsp;la&nbsp;Paz
