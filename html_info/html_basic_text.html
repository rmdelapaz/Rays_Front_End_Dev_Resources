Paragraphs and line breaks

Probably the most basic and most used tag is the p tag. Thats what we have for the majority of our text content.

Other HTML tags exist to provide markup identifying where the browser should have a line break, this is the br tag. Worth noting that the br tag similar to the img tag is closed by a slash right before the greater than sign of the br tag.
See sample below

<br />

The br tag is useful for addresses where you want several lines of text treated as one paragraph instead of separate elements.
