HyperText Markup Language: Basic Text Structure

Paragraphs and line breaks

Probably one of the most basic and most used tag is the p tag. Thats what we use for the majority of HTML text content.

HTML has markup identifying where the content should have a line break, this is the br tag. Worth noting that the br tag similar to the img tag does not jhave a closing tag. It is closed by a slash right before the greater than sign of the br tag. There will be a line break

Non Breaking spaces

Such was the concern for the sanctity and inviolate nature of some text, a markup to ensure that the space between two elements/content be always side by side. A good example of this is the name "de la Paz". By having the appropriate markup between de and la and between la and Paz, it will treat that last name as one unit instead of three separate potential lines. See code sample below.

de&nbsp;la&nbsp;Paz
