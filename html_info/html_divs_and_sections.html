HyperText Markup Language - div, span and section tags

What is div, span and section

Certain tags such as header and footer are simple and straight forward as to what they are and what the items are that are marking up while some such as div and section might be less obvious to their usage.

Something to keep in mind with HTML is that it's purpose it to "markup", "identify" or "tag" elements, or portions of a document. Semantically tagging content for differently abled people and/or SEO is just good practice and beneficial to all users.

Large sections of text or contant that need identifying or classification would use the section tag while a smaller division of text would use div and a small span of text would use the span tag
