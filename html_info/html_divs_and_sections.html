HyperText Markup Language - div and section tags

What is div and section

Certain tags such as header and footer are simple and straight forward as to what they are and what the items are that are marking up while some such as div and section might be less obvious to their usage.

Some mistakenly claim/think that HTML is a programming language. Um, it's in the name. It is a markup language that has no logic or algorithm. All it has is markup for the data structure.
