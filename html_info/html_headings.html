HyperText Markup Language Headings

What are they

Headings provide a way to organize you HTML document into logical sections. In HTML, there are six levels of headings beginning with h1, the largest to h6, the smallest. You should only use heading one once per page and use other subordinates headings as necessary and appropriately.
