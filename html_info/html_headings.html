HyperText Markup Language Headings

What are they

Headings provide a way to organize HTML documents into a logical hiearchy. In HTML, there are six levels of headings beginning with h1, the largest to h6, the smallest. You should only use heading one once per page and use other subordinates headings as necessary and appropriately.

When and Why

Two major reasons for why to properly use headings are Search Engine Optimazation (SEO) and for accessibility. Proper use of headings greatly help assistive reading devices for visually impaired users and anytime your website is optimizing its search engine result is a definite plus.
